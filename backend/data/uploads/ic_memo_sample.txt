INVESTMENT COMMITTEE MEMO

Date: May 11, 2026
Subject: Recommendation for Acquisition of Secondary LP Interest in NorthRiver IV

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
We recommend the acquisition of a secondary LP interest in NorthRiver IV at a 17% discount to the reported Net Asset Value (NAV). This investment offers exposure to a mature vintage with high-quality software and healthcare assets.

INVESTMENT RATIONALE
- Mature Vintage: The 2019 vintage is entering its peak harvesting period, suggesting near-term liquidity.
- High-Quality Assets: Significant concentration in software-as-a-service (SaaS) with strong margins and enterprise stability.
- GP Track Record: NorthRiver has a proven history of navigating middle-market buyouts and achieving successful exits.

KEY RISKS
- Concentration Risk: The top three assets represent approximately 45% of the remaining NAV.
- Leverage: Some portfolio companies carry higher leverage ratios which may be sensitive to interest rate fluctuations.
- Delayed Exits: Macroeconomic conditions may push anticipated exit timings into 2027.

KEY POSITIVES
- Strong Entry Point: The 17% discount provides a significant margin of safety.
- Valuation Support: Current marks appear conservative relative to recent public market comps in the tech sector.

RECOMMENDATION
Proceed with the purchase of the interest at the proposed 83% of NAV.
