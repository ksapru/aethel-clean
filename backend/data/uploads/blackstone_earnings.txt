Blackstone Inc. (BX) Q1 2026 Earnings Call Summary
Date: April 23, 2026

CALL PARTICIPANTS
- Stephen A. Schwarzman - Chairman and CEO
- Jon Gray - President and COO
- Michael Chae - CFO
- Weston Tucker - Head of Shareholder Relations

KEY HIGHLIGHTS
- Distributable Earnings: $1.8 billion, up 25% year-over-year.
- Fee-Related Earnings (FRE): Strong growth driven by management fees.
- Total Assets Under Management (AUM): Reached a record $1.3 trillion.
- Dividend: Declared a quarterly dividend of $1.16 per share.
- Inflows: Totaled $69 billion in the first quarter.
- Real Estate Performance: Positive appreciation in core portfolios; logistics and data centers remain top-performing sectors.
- Private Equity: Appreciation of 3.2% in the quarter; focus on high-conviction themes like AI infrastructure.
- Credit & Insurance: Significant growth in Private Credit; BCRED continues to see strong demand.
- Secondaries (Strategic Partners): Continued to see robust activity as LPs seek liquidity in a complex macro environment.

MANAGEMENT COMMENTARY
Stephen Schwarzman described the quarter as "outstanding," highlighting broad-based growth across all key metrics. Jon Gray noted that the firm's "all-weather" platform is performing well, with particular strength in infrastructure and data centers driven by the global AI build-out.

The firm emphasized its role as a key provider of liquidity through its secondaries and credit platforms. Management expects the exit environment to improve as interest rates stabilize and buyer-seller price gaps continue to narrow.
